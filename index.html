Got Questions?

We've got you covered with answers to the most common queries about the fest.

Nova Stellar is the premier annual cultural and technical festival of St. Francis De Sales College, Nagpur. It's a two-day mega-event bridging the gap between disruptive technology and profound cultural expression.

Nova Stellar takes place on 10th and 11th April, 2026. The event is hosted at the lush green campus of St. Francis De Sales College, Seminary Hills, Nagpur.

You can register directly on this site! Just head over to the "Secure Your Pass" section, fill out your details, and select whether you want to be a standard attendee or a participant for specific competitions.

Yes, passes are available for purchase. We offer Standard, VIP Pro (which includes the Fashion Show and Live Band Night Events), and Participant passes. Early birds get exclusive merch at the venue.
