Queries

Got Questions?

We've got you covered with answers to the most common queries about the fest.

Nova Stellar is a 2-day Intercollegiate Mega Fest organized by St. Francis De Sales College, Seminary Hills, Nagpur, featuring a variety of cultural, artistic, and intellectual competitions where students from different colleges can showcase their talent.

Nova Stellar 2026 will be held on 10th and 11th April 2026 at St. Francis De Sales College, Seminary Hills, Nagpur.

Students can participate in Nova Stellar 2026 by registering through the official event website. Participants need to visit the website, fill out the online registration form, select their preferred events, and complete the registration by submitting the required entry fee.

Yes, each event has a nominal entry fee depending on the category and participation type (solo, duet, or group). The fee details are available in the event registration section.
