Debate Rules & Regulations

Please read carefully before registering

1. Team Composition: This is a mandatory Two-Member Team event. One participant must argue FOR the motion, and the other must argue AGAINST the motion. (1 College = 1 Team).

2. Registration Fee: A fixed fee of ₹500 per team is applicable for both Intra and Inter-college participants. Registration fees once paid are strictly non-refundable under any circumstances.

3. Verification: Acceptance for the official WhatsApp group and the event will be granted only after successful UTR and payment screenshot verification.

4. Dress Code & ID: All participants must strictly wear their college uniform and carry a valid college ID card. Participation will not be allowed without valid identification.

5. Reporting Time: Participants must report to the College Auditorium by 9:15 AM sharp. The event will commence strictly at 10:00 AM.

6. Time Limits: In Round 1, each participant gets exactly 3 minutes to present their argument. Teams shortlisted for Round 2 will participate in a 40-50 minute rebuttal session.

7. Conduct & Decorum: Respectful and constructive discussion is mandatory. Any form of indecency, use of unparliamentary language, or violation of debate decorum will lead to immediate disqualification.

8. Final Authority: The decision of the judges, moderators, and the organizing committee will be final and binding in all matters.
