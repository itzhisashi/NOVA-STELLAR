Competition Rounds

Round 1: Preliminary

Each participant gets 3 minutes to present their argument. Teams will be shortlisted based on performance.

Round 2: Final Round (Rebuttal)

Focuses on rebuttals and interactive discussion. Total time: 40-50 minutes. Participants may challenge arguments and ask questions.

Participation Rules

  • 1 college = 1 team (2 students). One must argue FOR, the other AGAINST the motion.
  • Participants must wear their college uniform and carry their college ID card.
  • Respectful and constructive discussion is mandatory during rebuttals.
  • The decision of the judges and moderator is final and binding.

Judgement Criteria

Round 1 (Total: 100 Marks)

  • Content & Knowledge 20
  • Organization & Structure 20
  • Delivery & Presentation 20
  • Language & Expression 20
  • Originality and Creativity 20

Round 2 (Total: 50 Marks)

  • Listening & Comprehension 10
  • Rebuttal & Critical Thinking 10
  • Quick Thinking & Spontaneity 10
  • Clarity of Expression 10
  • Confidence & Delivery 10

  • 1st Runner-up: Shield + Certificate + Cash Prize
